Lexington Property Assessment Tool

This tool analyzes how a Lexington, MA property's 2024 assessment compares to similar homes in town, using data I scraped from the Town of Lexington's Public Assessor's Database. Enter a Lexington address to see your property's assessment against recent sales of similar houses, all town residences, and properties built around the same time with similar living area.
